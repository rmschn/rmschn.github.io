Team Core Values

March 14, 2022

For at least the last 12 years or more, I have shared the following Core Values with teams and places I have worked. Now to be quite honest, I do not recall any longer if I built these on top of something else, or if I just generated these out of thin air from my own thoughts. So any semblance to others is a happy accident of like mindedness. I’m quite sure over the years, I have modified the wording a bit. I’d be curious about additions or thoughts about these that people might have.

The following is the current set:

Core Values

People

  • Respect and care for well-being and professional development of yourself and others
  • Maintain atmosphere comprised of trust, empowerment, teamwork
  • Get the best people in the right place, including yourself

Courage

  • Take risk when necessary
  • Listen to our critics
  • Respond to the challenge and opportunity presented as it presents

Education

  • Recognize that life-long learning is a necessity
  • Provide and/or seek opportunity for further learning

Empowerment

  • Provide tools and information needed to create opportunity
  • Enable the decision maker, the innovator, the doer

Teamwork

  • Work together with common purpose, goals, creating a culture and atmosphere where everyone has a chance to contribute and achieve success as we strive towards delivering on our mission

Integrity

  • Say what you believe, do what you say! Be WYSIWYG!
  • Work with trust, honesty, caring, fairness, loyalty and diversity

Attitude

  • Reflects these values
  • Be enthusiastic, proactive and passionate

Accountability

  • By being accountable, you earn the right to hold others accountable. Execute driving success. Deliver!

Quality

  • Expect nothing but the best from you, your team and others around you
  • All of the above are necessary to achieve quality.
