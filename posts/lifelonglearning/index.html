Lifelong Learning

March 21, 2022

I’ve tried to write a post about lifelong learning repeatedly. I’m punting as every post I have started about it doesn’t measure up to what I would really like to say.

Lifelong Learning is a part of the experience.

I’m a lifelong learner. We all are, even if we do not recognize it yet. Some learn fast, some slow, some easy, some hard, some read, some do, some fake it, some act it, some listen for it, some have to speak it, other write, draw, paint, program, ride, etc. Lifelong learning is all of the things.

Lifelong learning isn’t really an option. Its what happens.

What people mean when they say they are a lifelong learner though is that they are purposeful about it. Its not an accident. Its not just passive. They actively seek it knowing they are seeking it.

In any case, I am a lifelong learner. You are one too!
