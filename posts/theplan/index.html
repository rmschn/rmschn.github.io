The Posting Plan

February 27, 2026

Brief History

I’ve never managed to be very consistent at doing this. I think it part its because I do not want to contribute to the mess that is online these days. That said, I find it incredibly fun to learn from others on the internet. So I keep revisiting this idea of writing regularly on my own site for the benefit of others. This is my latest attempt beginning here in 2026. I have a plan…

New Plan

So whats the new plan?

Well, consistency and scheduling. This site is going to be a place where I share ideas about technologies from a variety of view points, but mostly my own. These viewpoints will be informed by my own experiences and will likely come with some exposure to my sense of humor. :)

I’m going to try to keep it valuable to others. I’m going to try to keep it focused to technology and areas in technology that I have many years of experience and of course newer technologies where I am simply continually interested.

I am a life long learner and so I hope everyone that reads anything on this site gains from that time spent.

Tentative Schedule

So whats the schedule?

My current plan is to post weekly. Likely at the end of each week on a Friday. For now I’m going to leave it at that. I’m not going to set a time. Maybe later if this gets more organized.

So, if interested, plan on visiting on Fridays to check out whats been posted.

Thanks for Interest

Thanks for being here. Thanks for being interested. Hope to see you here next Friday for the first technical post!
