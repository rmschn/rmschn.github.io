A Brief Professional Bio

Ray’s experience ranges from the seed funded startup to the Fortune 500 inside telecoms, ISP’s financials, media and security companies. Ray is a technical leader and problem solver, technically focused and solutions oriented team builder. He is known for delivery and has a reputation of getting things done. His background includes time spent from all vantage points of the software development lifecycle (SDLC). He has worked in: software and product development, systems programming, technical operations, automation, modern virtualization and containerization platforms, various cloud providers in on-prem, hybrid, and public. Lastly, Ray has spent a good deal of his career working in the area of technical security from both internal systems programming and product development as well as leading R&D, quality assurance and customer support in various organizations.

Ray enjoys mentoring people and throughout his career some of these people have gone on to become Distinguished at some of the Fortune 500.
